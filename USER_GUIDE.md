# 🎓 Smart Kirana AI - User Guide

## For Shop Owners: Simple Step-by-Step Guide

### Getting Started (First Time)

#### Step 1: Phone Login
1. Open the app
2. Tap the phone input box
3. Enter your 10-digit mobile number
4. Tap **"Send OTP"** button (green)

**Demo Phone**: `9876543210`

#### Step 2: Verify OTP
1. You'll see OTP input screen
2. Enter: `123456` (demo code)
3. Tap **"Verify OTP"** button (green)

#### Step 3: Shop Name
1. Enter your shop name (e.g., "Kumar Kirana Store")
2. Tap **"Continue to App"** button

#### Step 4: Choose Language
Three big buttons will appear:
- 🇬🇧 **English** - Use English
- 🇮🇳 **हिंदी** - Use Hindi
- 🌟 **తెలుగు** - Use Telugu

Pick one and tap!

## 🎯 How to Use Features

### ➕ ADD A PRODUCT

1. Go to **Categories** tab
2. Tap **"Add Product"** (green button)
3. Fill in details:
   - **Product Name**: e.g., "Britannia Biscuits"
   - **Quantity**: e.g., "50"
   - **Unit**: App auto-selects (or choose: Pieces, Kg, Litres, Dozen)
   - **Expiry Date**: Optional (tap if needed)
4. Tap **"Save"** (green)

**Pro Tip**: App automatically knows units:
- Milk → Litres
- Rice → Kg
- Biscuits → Pieces
- Bread → Pieces

---

### 📊 RECORD A SALE

When you sell something:

1. Go to **Categories** or **Home**
2. Find the product card
3. Tap the product (tap on card)
4. Tap **"Record Sale"** (blue button)
5. Enter amount sold (e.g., "5")
6. Tap **"Record Sale"**

**App does this automatically**:
- ✅ Reduces stock
- ✅ Tracks sales history
- ✅ Calculates daily average
- ✅ Predicts when stock finishes

---

### 📈 STOCK PREDICTIONS

When you tap on a product, you see:

**"Stock Prediction"** (green box):
- Shows: "5 days left"
- Message: "Milk will run out in 5 days. Reorder now."

**How it works**:
- App looks at your sales from last 30 days
- Calculates average daily sales
- Predicts when stock finishes
- Tells you when to reorder (2 days before)

---

### 🔍 VIEW PRODUCT DETAILS

1. Find product in any tab
2. Tap the **product card**
3. See:
   - Category
   - Current stock
   - Expiry date
   - Stock prediction
   - When it was restocked

**Actions from detail screen**:
- 📊 **Record Sale** - Mark what you sold
- ✏️ **Edit** - Change quantity or expiry date
- 🗑️ **Delete** - Remove from inventory

---

### ⏰ MANAGE EXPIRY

Products with **expiry dates**:
- Shown with orange date badge
- Get warnings 2 days before expiry
- Marked in red if expiring today/tomorrow

**To set expiry**:
1. Tap **Add Product**
2. Fill in expiry date
3. Save

**Why it matters**:
- ⚠️ Prevents selling expired products
- 🤖 App warns you automatically
- 💰 Helps reduce waste

---

### 🚨 UNDERSTAND ALERTS

**Green Light** ✅
- Stock: Safe
- Action: None needed

**Yellow Light** ⚠️
- Stock: Low (5 days or less)
- Action: Plan to reorder soon

**Red Light** 🔴
- Stock: Critical (2 days or less)
- Action: REORDER NOW!
- Expiry: Expires soon (within 2 days)

## 💡 TIPS & TRICKS

### Quick Tips
1. **Big buttons are for you** - All buttons are large and easy to tap
2. **Icons help** - Use icons to understand what each button does
3. **Colors mean things**:
   - 🟢 Green = Good/Add/Save
   - 🟡 Yellow = Warning
   - 🔴 Red = Danger/Critical
   - 🔵 Blue = Info/Action

### Best Practices
- ✅ Record sales **every day** - Helps predictions be accurate
- ✅ Add expiry dates - Prevents expired stock
- ✅ Check alerts regularly - Don't miss reorder warnings
- ✅ Review products weekly - Keep inventory clean

### Saving Time
- Tap **Product Card** to quickly update stock
- Use **"Record Sale"** instead of manually updating
- Let app track daily sales - More accurate predictions

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I login next time?
A: Just enter your phone number again. App remembers you!

### Q: What if I forget the OTP?
A: Demo code is always `123456`. In real app, get it via SMS.

### Q: Can I change language?
A: Yes! Go to **Profile** tab and select new language.

### Q: How accurate are predictions?
A: More accurate if you record sales regularly. First week may vary.

### Q: What if stock prediction is wrong?
A: App learns from your sales pattern. More data = better predictions.

### Q: Can I edit a product?
A: Yes! Tap product → Tap **Edit** icon → Change quantity/expiry → Save

### Q: How do I delete a product?
A: Tap product → Tap **Delete** button (red) → Confirm

### Q: Do I need internet?
A: App works even without internet (data is saved locally).

### Q: What about old data?
A: All data is saved. You can see alerts anytime.
